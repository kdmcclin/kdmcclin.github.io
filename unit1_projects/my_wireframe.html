Wireframing My Site

This wireframe is a simple representation of the main areas on my site: container, header, nav, content, aside, and footer. Some of these sections contain (or will contain) other objects like lists, links, and images. My website with include links for blog posts, a footer with social network icons that link to my account, a navigation bar with links, and a header that might be an image or might be just text.

Note: after making this wireframe I shrunk the header, but as I forgot to save an editable version of it I've declined to remake it and have just left it as it originally was.
